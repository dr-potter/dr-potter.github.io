opinion: it is irrational to use the length of a work to criticize it (e.g. "what is up with all these 2 minute songs???")

the only correct length of a work is the shortest length that lets it fully tell its story

there is also the situation of wishing a work (movie, song, game, etc.) were longer because you enjoyed it so much. in that case, i would use that as evidence of the work's brilliance, not its fault. to me this means you felt so inspired by and connected to the work that you want more of it in your life
