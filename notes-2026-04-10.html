i've said this so many times to so many different people and i'm not going to stop:

those AI detectors that tell you if your writing sounds like it was generated by AI? yeah that doesn't work, and the very idea that it could ever work is inherently incorrect

the reason is literally how generative AI works; it's designed to produce something directly based on the data that was used to train it, and in this case, the training data is hundreds of millions of real written works created by humans... see the problem here?

AI writes like a human because that's literally what it's designed to do; if GPTZero says your essay is 100% AI, it could mean you had AI write it for you, but it could also mean you... just wrote it the way a normal human being would

there's no easy way to distinguish AI writing from human writing, because AI writing imitates human writing... this is actually really simple and yet a lot of you seem to choose not to understand it
