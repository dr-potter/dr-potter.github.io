by the way the quadratic formula is just the solution to the standard form of a quadratic equation when you complete the square

{ax}^2+bx+c=0

x^2+\frac{b}{a}x=-\frac{c}{a}

x^2+2\left(\frac{b}{2a}\right)x+\left(\frac{b}{2a}\right)^2=-\frac{c}{a}+\left(\frac{b}{2a}\right)^2

\left(x+\frac{b}{2a}\right)^2=-\frac{c}{a}+\frac{b^2}{4a^2}=\frac{b^2-4ac}{4a^2}

x+\frac{b}{2a}=\pm\sqrt{\frac{b^2-4ac}{4a^2}}=\pm\frac{\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}

x=\frac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}
