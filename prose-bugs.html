bugs

there is a girl who looks and sounds like every other girl. but this girl has bugs.

the bugs follow the girl around and they speak to her. the girl believes she is strong, she is loved, she is enough. but the bugs tell her she is weak, she is alone, she needs to try harder.

sometimes the bugs are loud. sometimes the bugs are quiet. but the bugs are always there. the girl does not agree with what the bugs are saying to her. but she must always listen to the bugs, because they are always with her.

sometimes the girl wonders if anyone else has bugs. the bugs tell her it doesn't matter.

sometimes the girl tries to get rid of the bugs. eventually, the bugs always find her again.

sometimes the girl tries to talk to other people about the bugs, hoping someone will understand. that is when the bugs are the loudest. and over and over again they tell her one thing:

"you are lying."
